Get in Touch
Commencing your journey with Panacea Home Care is a seamless process. You can begin by scheduling a complimentary consultation. Following this, our Care Coordinator will conduct a comprehensive home visit to assess specific requirements, address any concerns, and develop a personalized care strategy tailored to your family's needs.
We offer highly flexible scheduling options designed to accommodate diverse care requirements. Our services are available on an hourly, daily, or weekly basis, including dedicated 12-hour or 24-hour nursing shifts. All scheduling is subject to thorough clinical assessment and staff availability.
Excellence in care is our primary objective. Every member of our clinical team is rigorously trained, extensively experienced, and thoroughly verified. All professional nursing interventions are conducted by qualified health practitioners under strict medical supervision and established protocols.
To ensure the highest standards of evidence-based care, a valid doctor’s prescription is mandatory for all specialized medical and nursing interventions. For non-clinical assistance and basic caregiver support, a formal prescription may not be strictly required, depending on the scope of care.
